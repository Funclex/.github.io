About Us 关于嘉华

大连嘉华艺术发展有限公司是从事雕像设计及艺术铸造的专业化公司。自上世纪90年代成立以来，公司承担了国内外许多重大知名艺术工程的铸造。如上海黄浦公园“浦江潮”铜雕，深圳龙岗区政府广场“巨龙”铜雕，广州世界大观园“阿波罗”主雕，大连星海广场“百年城雕”，沈阳“九一八”纪念馆大型浮雕，珠海“圆明新园”铸铜群雕，苏州工业园区“海神”群雕以及美国佛罗里达州“美锦中华”主雕，亚特兰大“奥运龙塔”等。

1999年底，公司完成了著名的北京中华世纪坛长270米、宽3米的青铜甬道雕塑作品的铸造。这件气势恢宏的作品被中国艺术铸造专业委员会评为“20世纪末代表中国铸造最高艺术水平的标志性作品”。二十几年来，公司应邀完成了毛泽东、刘少奇、周恩来、朱德、邓小平等几十位老一辈革命家塑像的铸造。
 

Dalian Jiahua Art Development Co., Ltd. is a professional company engaged in the design and casting of sculpture. Since its establishment in 90s of last century, the company has undertaken a number of major domestic and international art projects. Such as Shanghai Huangpu Park "Pujiang tide" sculpture, Shenzhen Longgang district government square "Dragon", Guangzhou world garden "Appollo" the main carving, United States and Florida "Meijin" the main carving and Atlanta Olympic Tower etc. By the end of 1999, our company completed the famous Millennium Monument in Beijing which is 270 meters long and 3 meters wide. This magnificent work is called the end of twentieth Century on behalf of China casting the highest artistic level of iconic works award by the Art Casting Technical Committee of China Foundry Association. Over the past 20 years, the company was invited to complete the sculptures of Chairman Mao, Liu Shaoqi and hundreds of the old revolutionaries of China. We also have corporation with Japanese and German companies.
